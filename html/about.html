Safety & Hygine

Our Staffs are well experienced in several categories and trained to serve each and every passenger in our flights. Our top priority is always AVIATION SAFETY and CUSTOMER SAFETY because there are lives involved in every operation of aircraft. We care about our mother earth and believe in the protection of present and coming generations. We make sure to reduce the negative environmental impact of our functioning and highly believe in becoming a sustainable business solution in both the sky and on the ground. We have already taken numerous measures in the past months to protect our guests and employees in the current COVID-19 situation and are well prepared to handle any scenarios.

World Class Experience

CanGo Airways offers the best in the airline industry’s most ranges of in-flight cabins experience across its long-haul and short-haul networks. It is one of the worldwide airlines to offer four cabins on long-distance flights and remains strongly committed to providing unparallel experience in economy and premium class cabin. It means the airline can offer incomparable quality service and for the money fares for all customers.

Know more The CanGo airline implemented plans in 2009 in a major upgrade of the airline’s in-flight entertainment systems in all cabins. The introduction of ‘audio, visual contents’ means that customers can select a performance, movies or music content and control as they prefer during the flight, during rest, eat or work.
